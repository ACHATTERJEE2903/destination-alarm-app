Destination Alarm Prototype

Set a target location and get alerted when you are nearby.

Destination

Tracking

  • Status: Idle
  • Current location: Unknown
  • Distance to destination: --
  • Destination reached: No

How this prototype works

  1. Enter destination latitude/longitude and radius.
  2. Start tracking (requires location permission in your browser/device).
  3. The alarm fires once when you enter the radius.
